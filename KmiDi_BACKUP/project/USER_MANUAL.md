## What is KmiDi?

KmiDi is a music creation tool that helps you turn your emotions into music. Instead of needing to know music theory or play instruments, you describe how you feel, and KmiDi creates music that matches those emotions.

### What Can You Do With KmiDi?

- **Create music from emotions**: Tell KmiDi how you feel, and it creates music
- **Use in your favorite music software**: Works with Logic Pro, Ableton Live, and more
- **Create on any device**: Use on your computer, phone, or tablet
- **Express yourself**: Make music that truly represents your feelings

## Using the Desktop App

The Desktop App is the most powerful way to use KmiDi. It runs on your computer and gives you the most features.

### Installing the Desktop App

**For Mac:**
1. Download the KmiDi app from the website
2. Open the downloaded file
3. Drag the KmiDi icon to your Applications folder
4. Open KmiDi from Applications

**For Windows:**
1. Download the KmiDi installer from the website
2. Double-click the installer file
3. Follow the on-screen instructions
4. Click "Finish" when done
5. Find KmiDi in your Start menu and open it

### Your First Time Opening KmiDi

When you first open KmiDi, you'll see:

1. **Welcome Screen**: Click "Get Started" or "Skip Tutorial" if you want to explore on your own

2. **Main Screen**: This is where you'll create music. You'll see:
   - A text box where you describe your feelings
   - Buttons to generate music
   - A preview area to hear your music

### Creating Music in the Desktop App

**Step 1: Describe Your Feelings**
- Click in the text box at the top
- Type how you're feeling. Examples:
  - "I'm feeling sad and lonely"
  - "I want something energetic and happy"
  - "This is about missing someone I love"

**Step 2: Adjust the Settings (Optional)**
- **Motivation**: How driven or energetic you want the music (slider from low to high)
- **Chaos**: How unpredictable or experimental (slider from calm to wild)
- **Vulnerability**: How open and emotional (slider from guarded to raw)

**Step 3: Generate Your Music**
- Click the big "Generate Music" button
- Wait a few seconds while KmiDi creates your music
- You'll see a progress bar

**Step 4: Listen and Refine**
- Click the play button to hear your music
- If you want to change it:
  - Adjust the sliders
  - Change your description
  - Click "Generate Music" again

**Step 5: Save Your Work**
- Click "Save" in the top menu
- Give your song a name
- Choose where to save it
- Your song is now saved!

### Exporting Your Music

To use your music in other programs:

1. Click "Export" in the menu
2. Choose a format:
   - **MIDI File**: For use in music software (Logic Pro, Ableton, etc.)
   - **Audio File**: A finished song you can play anywhere
3. Choose where to save it
4. Click "Export"

## Using the Audio Plugins

Audio Plugins let you use KmiDi inside your favorite music software like Logic Pro, Ableton Live, or GarageBand.

### What Are Plugins?

Think of plugins as add-ons that give your music software new abilities. KmiDi plugins add emotional music creation to programs you might already use.

### Installing Plugins

**For Mac:**
1. Download the KmiDi plugins installer
2. Open the installer
3. Follow the instructions
4. The plugins will be installed automatically

**For Windows:**
1. Download the KmiDi plugins installer
2. Run the installer
3. Follow the on-screen instructions
4. Restart your music software

### Using Plugins in Your Music Software

**In Logic Pro:**
1. Open Logic Pro
2. Create a new track
3. Click on a track's "Insert" slot
4. Choose "KmiDi" from the plugin menu
5. The KmiDi plugin window will open
6. Use it just like the desktop app!

**In Ableton Live:**
1. Open Ableton Live
2. Create a new MIDI track
3. Find "Plug-ins" in the browser
4. Drag "KmiDi" onto your track
5. The plugin will open and you can use it

**In GarageBand:**
1. Open GarageBand
2. Create a new Software Instrument track
3. Click on the track's instrument slot
4. Choose "KmiDi" from the list
5. Start creating!

### Available Plugins

KmiDi includes 11 different plugins, each with a unique purpose:

1. **Pencil** - Sketch out rough musical ideas quickly
2. **Eraser** - Remove unwanted sounds or clean up audio
3. **Press** - Control the volume and dynamics (compression)
4. **Palette** - Change the color/tone of your music
5. **Smudge** - Blend sounds together smoothly
6. **Trace** - Create repeating patterns
7. **Parrot** - Repeat or mimic sounds
8. **Stencil** - Create sidechain effects (ducking)
9. **Chalk** - Add lo-fi, vintage character
10. **Brush** - Filter and shape sounds
11. **Stamp** - Create stutter and repeat effects

**Don't worry about understanding all of these!** Start with Pencil or Press - they're the easiest to use.

## Creating Your First Song

Let's walk through creating your very first song step by step.

### A Simple Example: "A Song About Missing Someone"

**Step 1: Open KmiDi**
- Open the Desktop App, Web App, or Mobile App

**Step 2: Describe Your Feeling**
- Type: "I'm missing someone I love. It's sad but also beautiful, like remembering good times."

**Step 3: Set Your Mood**
- **Motivation**: Set to medium (about 50%)
- **Chaos**: Set to low (about 20%) - we want it calm
- **Vulnerability**: Set to high (about 80%) - we want it emotional

**Step 4: Generate**
- Click "Generate Music"
- Wait 10-30 seconds

**Step 5: Listen**
- Click play
- Does it match what you're feeling?
- If not, try adjusting the sliders and generating again

**Step 6: Save**
- Click "Save"
- Name it "Missing You"
- Save it!

**Congratulations!** You've created your first song with KmiDi!

### Tips for Better Results

- **Be specific**: "I'm feeling sad" is okay, but "I'm feeling sad because I'm alone on a rainy day" is better
- **Use emotions, not instruments**: Don't say "I want a piano" - say "I want something gentle and intimate"
- **Experiment**: Try different combinations of settings
- **Don't give up**: If the first result isn't perfect, try again with different words

## Common Questions

### "Do I need to know music theory?"
**No!** KmiDi is designed for people who don't know music theory. Just describe your feelings.

### "Can I use this in my music software?"
**Yes!** Install the plugins and use KmiDi in Logic Pro, Ableton Live, GarageBand, and more.

### "Is my music saved automatically?"
**No** - you need to click "Save" to save your work. The app will remind you if you try to close without saving.

### "Can I edit the music after it's created?"
**Yes!** You can:
- Regenerate with different settings
- Export to MIDI and edit in music software
- Use the plugins to modify sounds

### "How much does it cost?"
Check the KmiDi website for current pricing. There's usually a free version to try!

### "Can I use this commercially?"
Check the license agreement, but typically yes - you own the music you create.

### "What file formats can I export?"
- MIDI files (for music software)
- WAV files (high quality audio)
- MP3 files (smaller, for sharing)

### "Does it work offline?"
- Desktop App: Yes, mostly
- Web App: No, needs internet
- Mobile App: Some features work offline

### "Can I collaborate with others?"
Yes! The web app supports sharing and collaboration features.

## Troubleshooting

### "The app won't open"

**Try this:**
1. Make sure your computer meets the requirements
2. Restart your computer
3. Reinstall the app
4. Check if your antivirus is blocking it

**Still not working?** Contact support with your computer type and error message.

### "Music generation is slow"

**This is normal!** Creating music takes 10-60 seconds depending on:
- How complex your description is
- Your internet speed (for web app)
- Your computer's speed

**To speed it up:**
- Use simpler descriptions
- Close other programs
- Use the desktop app instead of web app

### "The music doesn't sound right"

**Try:**
1. Be more specific in your description
2. Adjust the sliders (Motivation, Chaos, Vulnerability)
3. Try different words to describe the same feeling
4. Generate multiple times - each one is different

**Remember:** Music creation is an art. Sometimes you need to try a few times to get it right!

### "I can't find my saved songs"

**Check:**
1. Click "Open" or "Load" in the app
2. Check your Documents folder
3. Check the app's "My Songs" section
4. If using web app, check "My Cloud Songs"

### "The plugin won't show up in my music software"

**Try:**
1. Make sure you installed the plugins correctly
2. Restart your music software
3. Check that your music software supports plugins
4. Reinstall the plugins

### "The web app won't load"

**Try:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different web browser
3. Clear your browser's cache
4. Try again later (the server might be busy)

### "I'm getting error messages"

**Don't panic!** Error messages are usually easy to fix:
- **"Connection failed"**: Check your internet
- **"Not enough memory"**: Close other programs
- **"File not found"**: Make sure you saved your work
- **"Plugin error"**: Reinstall the plugins

**Still stuck?** Write down the exact error message and contact support.

## Quick Reference Guide

### Desktop App Shortcuts

- **Generate Music**: Click the big button or press `Space`
- **Play/Pause**: Click play button or press `Space`
- **Save**: `Cmd+S` (Mac) or `Ctrl+S` (Windows)
- **New Song**: `Cmd+N` (Mac) or `Ctrl+N` (Windows)
- **Open Song**: `Cmd+O` (Mac) or `Ctrl+O` (Windows)

### Understanding the Settings

**Motivation Slider:**
- Low = Calm, relaxed, peaceful
- High = Energetic, driven, intense

**Chaos Slider:**
- Low = Predictable, structured, traditional
- High = Experimental, unpredictable, wild

**Vulnerability Slider:**
- Low = Guarded, controlled, reserved
- High = Open, emotional, raw
