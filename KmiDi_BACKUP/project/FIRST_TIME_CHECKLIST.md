## Your First Song

### Step 1: Describe Your Feeling
- [ ] I typed how I'm feeling in the text box
- [ ] I was specific (e.g., "sad because I'm alone" not just "sad")

### Step 2: Generate Music
- [ ] I clicked/tapped "Generate Music"
- [ ] I waited for it to finish (10-30 seconds is normal)

### Step 3: Listen
- [ ] I clicked/tapped the play button
- [ ] I listened to my creation
- [ ] I decided if I like it or want to try again

### Step 4: Save Your Work
- [ ] I clicked/tapped "Save"
- [ ] I gave my song a name
- [ ] I confirmed it saved successfully
